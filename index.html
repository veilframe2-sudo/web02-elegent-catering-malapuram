№ 04 — Our Story

A kitchen rooted
in Munduparamba.

We were started by a small family of cooks who had spent two decades inside the bigger Malappuram wedding kitchens — and wanted a kitchen of our own that would not cut corners on the spice tin or on the stewards' wages. That is still the rule.

Every event is led by one of three head chefs from start to finish — you will never be passed to a stranger on the day of the wedding. The biryani is opened, the sadya is laid, and the family eats first.

26 Sadya dishes A full Onam-grammar spread on the banana leaf.
12 Years of work Founded 2014. Munduparamba kitchen since day one.
1:18 Service ratio One trained steward for every eighteen guests.
100% Halal sourced All meats sourced & certified. Vegetarian kept separate.
